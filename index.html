How to Play

Two teams: Hiders vs Seekers. Equal stats — pure skill wins. No RNG, no power-ups.

Hiding Phase

  • Each round starts with a 10 second hiding phase.
  • Seekers are frozen. Hiders scatter and find cover.

Hider Goals

  • Use bushes as visual cover — they block line of sight, not coded detection.
  • Seekers must aim their tag (60° cone, 2m range) — no cheap radius tags.
  • Free hiders touch Jail to free all prisoners!
  • Survive the clock to win.

Seeker Goals

  • Find and tag all hiders before time runs out.
  • Tags require aiming — face your target within the cone.
  • Guard the Jail to prevent jailbreaks.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrows — Move
  • Shift — Sprint
  • Space — Hide in bushes
  • 1 Dash (4s) · 2 Vanish 15s / Tracker 20s · 3 Pulse 16s
  • Q — Ping / Callout
  • Juke — 180° flick while sprinting = 1.5× speed for 0.5s
  • Tab — Spectate (when jailed) · Esc — Pause
  • Mobile: Joystick + action buttons. Swipe to juke.

Abilities

  • Dash (4s) — Burst of speed forward
  • Vanish (Hiders, 15s) — Brief invisibility
  • Tracker (Seekers, 20s) — Reveal hider footprints for 5s
  • Pulse (16s) — Reveal enemies on minimap

Best of 3

  • Survive = win + hide again. All jailed = seekers win + teams swap.
  • First to 2 wins. 2-0 Sweep / 2-1 Clutch.

Cosmetics

  • Glove skins — Your first-person hands
  • Jailbreak FX — Massive explosion everyone sees
  • Purely visual. No gameplay advantage.
